the year 2016

I went to a frat party last Saturday hoping to meet my prince Charming. I mean there's 50 boys living in one house, how hard could this be?

As I expected, frat bros are the most romantic boys in the word! I was immediately swept off my feet when one asked me if I wanted to take a Burnetts handle pull.

From the kindness of his heart, he even gave me the option of Strawberry Banana, Tropical Fruit, and Pink Lemonade. It was so sweet; I took a pull of them all.

Just the fact that he could afford 3 bottles of the finest and most luxurious vodka made me begin to think of the life this man could show me. I had to know his name. I asked and the answer made me melt- "Chad."

But that's when I saw Todd. He was wearing nothing but a furry speedo, cowboy hat, with cowboy boots swinging around a 12 inch gold magic wand. That's when I remembered the last time I wanted to buy a 12 inch magic wand. They cost $2000 each. Todd was def richer.

At that moment I knew I wanted Todd. I went up to him and he told me he got it from Diddy.

Unsure if it was just me or all the burnetts going to my head, but the whole room turned into a set of a 2000's rap and hip hop music video. It was pretty hot.

I kept my eyes on Todd and his 12 inch funstick from the table I was twerking upon. Then 50 Cent approached and offered me a tab of "ratchet acid"- his own proprietary formula. I just couldn't say no.

But THAT's when I saw Dick with a ham and cheese sandwich. I ran towards him and snatched the sandwich out of his hand, quickly eating it all before he could react

The only thing that was missing was the spicy ranch.

That's when I slid to the kitchen to raid the fridge. The only thing in there was half a bottle of jager, a rotten banana, some expired milk, and a tub of ice cream with a screwdriver in it. I said forget this and eyed the room for souvenirs to steal.

Todd walked in with Flava Fla by his side. I knew only one thing could make this moment perfect. And there it was, in Flav's beautiful arms- a gallon of spicy ranch. And just in the nick of time (according to his clock), the acid kicked in.

The next morning I woke up covered in spicy ranch with Flava Flav doing my dishes and 50 Cent vacuuming my floor. I #slayed.

...and that kids, is how I met your father.
